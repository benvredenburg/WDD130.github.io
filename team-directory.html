Team Directory

Lisa Baxter Hong, Occupational Therapist

Education

Lisa Baxter Hong is a board-certified Occupational general therapist with a specialty area in dementia care. She has completed research in the area of caregiver education and spends time with the Alzheimer’s Association where she serves on the local board as the care a support chair. She is a member of both state and national OT associations and is passionate about advancing the profession. She loves working as an OT across the lifespan to help people achieve independence and meaning. She earned her Occupational Therapy Doctorate from Eastern Kentucky University and has been practicing since 2007.

Mandi Eck, Occupational Therapist

Education

Mandi Eck is a board-certified Occupational Therapist with experience treating a wide range of disorders/diagnoses. She treats clients from birth to adulthood. She has extensive experience treating infants with torticollis. Her training includes sensory processing/integration, myofascial techniques, NDT (neuro-developmental treatment) and torticollis. She earned her Master’s degree in Occupational Therapy from Idaho State University and has been practicing since 2005.
